Company

GlobalMart Distribution

Industry

Supply Chain & Logistics

Problem Statement

Executives lack visibility into supplier performance, warehouse utilization, logistics costs, and inventory risk. Existing reports describe historical performance but fail to predict future stockouts or identify operational bottlenecks.

Project Objective

Develop a Supply Chain Control Tower that enables executives to monitor operational health, identify bottlenecks, evaluate supplier performance, and proactively reduce stockout risk through predictive inventory insights.
